The midterm lab grading for Programming 2 is split into two parts. The lecture grade is 60% from the exam and 40% from class standing (quizzes and attendance, with a 10% deduction per absence, failing after 4). The lab grade is similarly divided: 60% from exams (various Java and JS assessments) and 40% from class standing (lab work and attendance).
